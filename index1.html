Jubin Nautiyal

Jubin Nautiyal is an Indian singer. Jubin was awarded Upcoming Male Vocalist of the Year at 8th Mirchi Music Awards, 2016 for his song "Zindagi Kuch Toh Bata (Reprise)" from Bajrangi Bhaijaan, another one of his achievements is the Rising Musical Star Award (2015) received at Zee Business Awards. Early in his career, he sang many songs, including several hit songs, for Hindi films.
